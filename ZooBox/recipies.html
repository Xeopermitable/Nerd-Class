Recipie: Fried Gator (from foodnetwork.com)

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound alligator meat, cut into chunks
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • Flour, for dredging
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1 cup hot sauce
  • 1 bottle store bought ranch dressing, for dipping
Steps
  1. Heat a deep-fryer to 350˚F.
  2. Lightly season gator meat with salt and pepper prior to dredging them in flour.
  3. Combine buttermilk and hot sauce into 1 mixture.
  4. Dip the gator meat into the buttermilk and hot sauce mixture and dip, once again, in flour.
  5. Then place in deep fryer until golden brown, just a couple minutes. Drain on paper towels and serve with ranch dressing.
  6. Enjoy!
